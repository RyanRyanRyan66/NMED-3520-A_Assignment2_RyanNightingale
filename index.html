Why Time Zones

The course that introduced me to this topic was GEOG-1000 Introduction to Physical Geography. The amount of time we spent learning about time zones was very short but it inspired me to take a closer look on my own time. I was shocked to learn that time zones aren't boring at all, in fact they are incredibly interesting. I hope after reading this website you can experience the same shock that I did when first diving into the time zone zone.

By: Ryan Nightingale
