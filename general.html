How AI is Impacting Web Design/Development

I think AI is impacting web design/development in a more positive than negative way. AI is helping reduce the amount of manual coding efforts needed for creating a website by making this process quicker, easier, and more streamlined. This allows people to spend more time and effort on the creative aspects of their project and making their website the best it can possibly be. Overall, I believe AI in web development is preserving human creativity and even nurturing it.

By: Ryan Nightingale
